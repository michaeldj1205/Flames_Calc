📜 How FLAMES Works?

FLAMES stands for:

🔥 F – Friends
❤️ L – Lovers
💖 A – Affection
💍 M – Marriage
⚔️ E – Enemies
👫 S – Siblings

The game works by removing common letters from both names and counting the remaining ones. This number determines the relationship type using FLAMES.
